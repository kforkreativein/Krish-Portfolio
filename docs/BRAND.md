## Tone of Voice

Overall feel: Edgy, creative, unconventional — stands out from every generic Indian freelancer portfolio
Write like: A confident creative who knows exactly what they're doing. Not cocky, not salesy — just certain.

DO use:
- Short, punchy sentences
- Confident statements ("I build content that converts" not "I try to help brands")
- Specific numbers and results where possible
- Industry language that signals expertise
- Second person — speak directly to the visitor ("your brand", "you")

DO NOT use:
- Generic phrases: "passionate about", "dedicated to", "helping businesses thrive"
- Indian agency clichés: "we provide best quality service"
- Excessive exclamation marks
- Vague claims without backing
- Long paragraphs — keep it tight

Voice examples:

WRONG: "I am passionate about video editing and dedicated to helping your business grow."
RIGHT: "I make content that stops the scroll. Every frame is intentional. Every edit has a reason."

WRONG: "We provide best in class social media management services."
RIGHT: "Your competitors are posting. You should be converting."
